Our Story
❧
The Bride
Neha Sri S R
From the serene town of Sathyamangalam, nestled at the foothills of the Nilgiris, Neha Sri is a soul as genuine as the jasmine she adorns. A devoted classical dancer, her every movement tells a story — of devotion, of grace, of love given freely.
"நடனமாடும் போது அவள் தெய்வமாகிறாள்"In her dance, she becomes divine
Her Heart Says
"He is steady, sincere, and home all at once. Love does not need grand gestures when it is this real."
The Groom
Kavinnesan V P
Born in Erode and now building dreams across the skyline of Sydney, Kavinnesan carries the pride of his construction family with quiet strength. He has built towers — but it is Neha Sri who has built a home in his heart.
"கட்டிடம் கட்டலாம், ஆனால் காதலை கட்டுவது கடினம் — அவளால் அது எளிதானது"Buildings can be built, but love is hard to build — she made it easy
His Heart Says
"From Erode to Sydney, I have travelled far — but every road led me back to her. She is everything I never knew I was searching for."
